Power Chains

"Power Chains" may sound scary but it is not as scary as it sounds. When there is space, a Power Chains is used. It is an elastic tie that goes around the brackets where it will close the space.

Filling A Cavity

Let's watch a full cavity filling procedure at the dentist! This cavity is a 2-surface cavity, meaning two surfaces of the tooth needs to be drilled and filled (one surface being in-between two teeth). This type of filling can also be called an interproximal filling.

Teeth Whitening

This video shows what to expect when getting your teeth professionally whitened at the dentist. If you want white teeth and you want them fast, in-office whitening is probably one of your best options!
